Mirchi

I had visited this wonderful place in April 2017

Our menu items include the most tantalising and flavoursome Indian cuisine. Papadums with spicy chutneys, mixed pickles, and Hyderabadi biryani and lamb dishes are the most outstanding dishes of Mirchi. Our signature dishes stand best among all. It includes all types of curry and tikkas. Prawns, fish curry, Dosas, karahi and special Indian beards. A separate menu is also available for kids like nuggets, fish fingers, veg rolls and fried rice etc. A special menu for festive has tandoori chicken, desi murgh saag paneer etc. We serve the best Indian classical curry and traditional tandoori grills including a variety of drinks like mixers, vodka, soft drinks and cocktails. No event is complete without a desert hence we provide fresh and appetising cultural deserts and the most famous.

Excellent film to culminate MCU ride

Avengers: Endgame” is the culmination of a decade of blockbuster filmmaking, the result of years of work from thousands of people. It is designed to be the most blockbuster of all the blockbusters, a movie with a dozen subplots colliding, and familiar faces from over 20 other movies.

Amazing movie if your up for a devastating twist

It's not so much that the Earth is destroyed, but that it's done so thoroughly.
